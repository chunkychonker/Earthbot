About

Earth Bot is an informational bot that gives users info on local environmental issues. Earth bot informs people about their community's environmental data and provides them with volunteer opportunities.

Our mission is to raise awareness about environmental concerns and encourage individuals to take action to protect the planet. Through our bot, we aim to provide easily accessible information to empower people to make environmentally conscious choices.

Join us in our efforts to create a sustainable future for generations to come.
